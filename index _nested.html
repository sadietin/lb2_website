The dawning of a new Reformation will reveal The Eternal Reformer THE HOLY SPIRIT.
The Reformation of Martin Luther to the church of the day brought “The Just Shall Live By Faith”
The New Reformation will Enlighten the body of Christ to the truth that “We have been made the righteousness of God In Christ Jesus” And are no longer sinners saved by Grace but Are NOW Saints Saved by Grace. II Corinthians 5:21

The Holy Spirit is signifying this, that the way into the holy place has not yet been disclosed while the outer tabernacle is still standing which is a symbol for the present time. Accordingly both gifts and sacrifices are offered which cannot make the worshiper perfect in conscience, since they relate only to food and drink and various washings, regulations for the body imposed until a time of reformation.

Hebrews 9:8-10
